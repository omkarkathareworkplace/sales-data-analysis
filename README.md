## 📌 Project Overview
Understanding sales performance is critical for optimizing inventory, targeting marketing campaigns, and forecasting future revenue. 

This project cleans, processes, and analyzes raw transactional sales data to extract actionable business intelligence. Through data visualisations and statistical summary techniques, we highlight high-performing product categories, peak sales periods, and customer value metrics.

---

## ❓ Key Business Questions Answered
1. What are the top-selling products and highest revenue-generating categories?
2. How do sales fluctuate over time (monthly, quarterly, and seasonal patterns)?
3. What is the average order value (AOV) and overall revenue growth rate?
4. Which customer segments or geographic regions contribute most to revenue?
